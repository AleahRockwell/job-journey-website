What is Job Journey?

Empowering Youth, Building Futures

Job Journey is a comprehensive 12-week employment readiness program developed by CanAssist at the University of Victoria. We empower youth ages 16+ with disabilities to build the skills, confidence, and experience they need to enter the workforce.

Our unique approach combines individualized career planning, hands-on skill building, real-world experience, and ongoing mentorship to ensure lasting success for both participants and employer partners.
